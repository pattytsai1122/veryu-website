About this experience

Hexmoor is a hidden wizarding prison built into a Hackney Road unit — from the outside it's just another shopfront; inside, you're processed as a new inmate and locked into a 1h45m live story with actors, guards, and a system that isn't as controlled as it first looks.

Three tiers are available — Apprentice, Enchanter and Sorcerer — each including a set of handcrafted cocktails or mocktails woven into the plot as contraband potions.

It's from the same production team behind Alcotraz, Avora and Moonshine Saloon, so the bar for set design and cast quality is consistent across all four.
