About this experience

Skuna's hot tub boats cruise through Canary Wharf while you sit in a private, wood-fired hot tub — self-driven, so it's your group and your route within the marina.

Water is fresh every single trip and heated to perfection; there's a cooler option for warmer months. Sessions run 75 minutes in the water with a 20-minute induction beforehand, and children 12+ are welcome with an adult.

Prefer someone else to steer? The Hot Tub Guided Tour (£39pp) is a 75-minute guided route finished with a glass of bubbles.
